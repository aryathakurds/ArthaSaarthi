# Bank Product Knowledge Base

## Savings Account
A savings account is suitable for keeping money liquid and accessible. It offers lower interest but high flexibility.

Sample prototype rate: 3.0% per annum.

Best for:
- Emergency liquidity
- Daily transactions
- Short-term cash needs

Risk level: Low  
Liquidity: High  
Lock-in: None

## Sweep-in Fixed Deposit
A sweep-in fixed deposit automatically moves excess balance into a deposit while keeping withdrawal flexibility.

Sample prototype rate: 6.8% per annum.

Best for:
- Idle balance optimization
- Emergency fund support
- Customers who want better returns without fully locking money

Risk level: Low  
Liquidity: Medium to High  
Lock-in: Flexible, depending on bank rules

## Recurring Deposit
A recurring deposit helps customers save a fixed amount every month.

Sample prototype rate: 6.5% per annum.

Best for:
- Goal-based disciplined saving
- Conservative customers
- Short to medium-term goals

Risk level: Low  
Liquidity: Medium  
Lock-in: Bank-defined tenure

## SIP / Mutual Fund Investment
A systematic investment plan allows customers to invest regularly in market-linked products.

Best for:
- Long-term wealth creation
- Retirement
- Home down payment
- Child education

Risk level: Depends on fund type  
Liquidity: Depends on product  
Important: Returns are not guaranteed.

## Tax Saving Bucket
Tax-saving investments should be suggested only after checking:
- Existing 80C usage
- Lock-in comfort
- Risk profile
- Goal timeline
- Customer consent

The assistant must not promise tax savings without confirming eligibility and applicable law.
