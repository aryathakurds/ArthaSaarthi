AI-powered digital wealth management for banks

Personalized wealth guidance for every banking customer

ArthaSaarthi AI helps banks deliver simple, secure, and personalized financial guidance using customer behavior, spending patterns, and bank-verified intelligence.

Prototype Demo Meet AI Agent

Wealth management is still difficult for many banking customers because advice is often fragmented, delayed, or available only to selected high-value users.

ArthaSaarthi AI solves this by converting everyday banking behavior into meaningful financial guidance. It studies spending habits, idle balances, savings goals, loan eligibility, risk profile, and investment behavior to give timely recommendations inside the bank's mobile app.

The system also helps during unusual transactions. If a customer makes a payment that does not match their normal behavior, the AI assistant can immediately ask for bank-verified confirmation using MPIN, OTP, biometric, or JWT-based authentication.

The future is human plus AI.

Meet ArthaSaarthi, the bank's intelligent wealth companion.

ArthaSaarthi works like a trusted digital advisor inside the bank app. It explains what customers can save, where they can invest, whether they should take a loan, and how each decision may affect their future financial health.

The agent does not blindly push products. Every suggestion is checked through customer suitability, bank rules, risk profile, and Trust Layer AI before any sensitive financial action is recommended.

  • Personalized wealth advice based on real banking behavior
  • Goal-based planning for savings, investment, and loans
  • Loan what-if support before taking major financial decisions
  • Instant payment confirmation for unusual transactions
